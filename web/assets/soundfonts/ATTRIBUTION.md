# Acoustic preview sample attribution

BandForge's browser preview uses a curated subset of the FluidR3 General MIDI
SoundFont: acoustic grand piano, steel-string acoustic guitar, fingered electric
bass, and the standard drum kit.

- Original SoundFont: `FluidR3_GM.sf2`, by Frank Wen and contributors.
- License: [Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/).
- License/source record: [gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts](https://github.com/gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts#soundfonts-available).
- Conversion: the selected presets were converted to `sf2-json` sample data by
  the WebAudioFonts preset service and downloaded on 2026-07-22.
- Local preset IDs: `0000_FluidR3`, `0252_FluidR3`, `0330_FluidR3`, and
  `12830_FluidR3`.
